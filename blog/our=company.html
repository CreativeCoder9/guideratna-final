About us

GuideRatna, a Govt. recognized startup company, disrupting the way tourist normally buy the experience. It is an app based platform that pair solo and group tourist with local tourist guide and guided experience.This patented innovation, potentially disrupt the travel and tourism Industry globally.

Our Foundation

Founded in [2018 by  R Biswas, GuideRatna has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. Our founder Ratan Biswas passion for travelling always drove him to  visit various tourist spots , where he noticed a lack of a platform where people can find find tourist guides easily., And that gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into building 'GuideRatna'. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the global tourismindustry. We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
