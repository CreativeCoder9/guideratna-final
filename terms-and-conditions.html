CANCELLATION POLICY

A Traveler may cancel a Booked Tour, as per below schedule:

Spot Booking

-6 hrs before schedule booking, Full refund however Rs. 100 or equivalent will be deducted as administrative fees-1 hrs before schedule booking, 50% refund-No refund in case cancellation after 1hr of scheduled booking.

City Booking

-6 hrs before schedule booking, Full refund however Rs. 100 will be deducted as administrative fees-1 hrs before schedule booking, 50% refund-No refund in case cancellation after 1hr of scheduled booking.

Book later

6 hrs before schedule booking, Full refund however Rs. 100 will be deducted as administrative fees-1 hrs before schedule booking, 50% refund-No refund in case cancellation after 1hr of schedule booking

Refund

A Traveler shall apply or call for the refund request. Refund will be as per above condition and pay by 15 working days to respective bank ac.
